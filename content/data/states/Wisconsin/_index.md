##  Wisconsin

Excluding public employees and multi-union election, in the last 12 months there have been 21 union elections filed in Wisconsin and 15 union elections held. In 11 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 3 open representation cases and 2 of are still waiting to vote.
