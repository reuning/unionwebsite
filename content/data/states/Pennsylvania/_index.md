##  Pennsylvania

Excluding public employees and multi-union election, in the last 12 months there have been 83 union elections filed in Pennsylvania and 68 union elections held. In 51 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 14 open representation cases and 12 of are still waiting to vote.
