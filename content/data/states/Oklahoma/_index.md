##  Oklahoma

Excluding public employees and multi-union election, in the last 12 months there have been 10 union elections filed in Oklahoma and 7 union elections held. In 4 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 3 open representation cases and 3 of are still waiting to vote.
