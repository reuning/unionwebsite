##  Massachusetts

Excluding public employees and multi-union election, in the last 12 months there have been 81 union elections filed in Massachusetts and 51 union elections held. In 38 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 26 open representation cases and 19 of are still waiting to vote.
