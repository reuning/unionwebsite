##  UNITE HERE

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 10 union elections filed by the UNITE HERE and 8 union elections held. In 6 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 4 open representation cases and 2 of are still waiting to vote.
