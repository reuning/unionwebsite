##  AFSCME

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 35 union elections filed by the AFSCME and 27 union elections held. In 24 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 15 open representation cases and 13 of are still waiting to vote.
