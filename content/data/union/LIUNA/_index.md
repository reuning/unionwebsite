##  LIUNA

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 10 union elections filed by the LIUNA and 9 union elections held. In 3 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 6 open representation cases and 4 of are still waiting to vote.
