##  Teamsters

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 210 union elections filed by the Teamsters and 211 union elections held. In 123 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 55 open representation cases and 32 of are still waiting to vote.
