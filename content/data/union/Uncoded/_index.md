##  Uncoded

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 616 union elections filed by the Uncoded and 119 union elections held. In 75 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 73 open representation cases and 47 of are still waiting to vote.
