##  BCTGM

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 8 union elections filed by the BCTGM and 4 union elections held. In 2 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 5 open representation cases and 5 of are still waiting to vote.
