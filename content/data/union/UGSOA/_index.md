##  UGSOA

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 4 union elections filed by the UGSOA and 3 union elections held. In 2 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 4 open representation cases and 4 of are still waiting to vote.
