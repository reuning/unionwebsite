##  NewsGuild

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 5 union elections filed by the NewsGuild and 7 union elections held. In 6 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 2 open representation cases and 1 of are still waiting to vote.
