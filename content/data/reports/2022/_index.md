## 2022 filings

This report details the number of filings and closed units in 2022. As a reminder, we are only showing filings and closures related to single union elections (rare multi-union elections are excluded).

### Filings by Union
In 2022 930 petitions for new units were filed, a -31.31% decrease from the previous year The median size was 22 with a total of 48,851 workers across all units, a -38.98% decrease from the previous year

### Closed Elections by Union
In 2022 510 petitions for new units were closed (this includes petitions where no election was ever held), with 48.63% closed with a certification order, creating 248 total new units. This was a -61.49% decrease from the previous year in successful union certifications. Overall this represents approximately 12,963 workers, which is a -58.07% decrease from the previous year The median bargaining unit has 17 workers.
