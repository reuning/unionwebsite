## 2019, 3rd Quarter filings

This report details the number of filings and closed units in the 3rd quarter of 2019. As a reminder, we are only showing filings and closures related to single union elections (rare multi-union elections are excluded).

### Filings by Union
In the 3rd quarter of 2019 442 petitions for new units were filed, a -8.68% decrease from the previous quarter The median size was 21 with a total of 24,029 workers across all units, a -1.03% decrease from the previous quarter

### Closed Elections by Union
In the 3rd quarter of 2019 457 petitions for new units were closed (this includes petitions where no election was ever held), with 52.74% closed with a certification order, creating 241 total new units. This was a 10.05% increase from the previous quarter in successful union certifications. Overall this represents approximately 10,040 workers, which is a -11.79% decrease from the previous quarter The median bargaining unit has 16 workers.
