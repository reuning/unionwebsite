## 2019, 2nd quarter filings

This report details the number of filings and closed units in the 2nd of 2019. As a reminder, we are only showing filings and closures related to single union elections (rare multi-union elections are excluded).

### Filings by Union
In the 2nd quarter of 2019, 370 units were filed, a 27.84% increase from the previous quarter. The median size was 18 with a total of 17,842 workers across all units, a 18.69% increase from the previous quarter.

### Closed Elections by Union
In the 2nd quarter of 2019, 335 units were closed, with 66.57% closed with a certification order, creating 223 total new units. This was a 21.52% increase from the previous quarter in successful union certifications. Overall this represents approximately 11,830 workers, which is a 25.16% increase from the previous quarter. The median bargaining unit has 21 workers.
