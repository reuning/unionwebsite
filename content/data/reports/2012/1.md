## 2012, 1st Quarter filings

This report details the number of filings and closed units in the 1st quarter of 2012. As a reminder, we are only showing filings and closures related to single union elections (rare multi-union elections are excluded).

### Filings by Union
In the 1st quarter of 2012 510 petitions for new units were filed, a 19.16% increase from the previous quarter The median size was 24 with a total of 30,208 workers across all units, a 1.39% increase from the previous quarter

### Closed Elections by Union
In the 1st quarter of 2012 491 petitions for new units were closed (this includes petitions where no election was ever held), with 39.10% closed with a certification order, creating 192 total new units. This was a -19.33% decrease from the previous quarter in successful union certifications. Overall this represents approximately 11,063 workers, which is a -34.94% decrease from the previous quarter The median bargaining unit has 23 workers.
