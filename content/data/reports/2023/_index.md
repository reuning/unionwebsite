## 2023 filings

This report details the number of filings and closed units in 2023. As a reminder, we are only showing filings and closures related to single union elections (rare multi-union elections are excluded).

### Filings by Union
In 2023 1,928 petitions for new units were filed, a -9.61% decrease from the previous year The median size was 20 with a total of 118,294 workers across all units, a -10.81% decrease from the previous year

### Closed Elections by Union
In 2023 1,757 petitions for new units were closed (this includes petitions where no election was ever held), with 56.06% closed with a certification order, creating 985 total new units. This was a -11.50% decrease from the previous year in successful union certifications. Overall this represents approximately 74,647 workers, which is a 32.61% increase from the previous year The median bargaining unit has 20 workers.
