## 2023 filings

This report details the number of filings and closed units in 2023. As a reminder, we are only showing filings and closures related to single union elections (rare multi-union elections are excluded).

### Filings by Union
In 2023 977 petitions for new units were filed, a -54.20% decrease from the previous year The median size was 21 with a total of 66,427 workers across all units, a -49.96% decrease from the previous year

### Closed Elections by Union
In 2023 855 petitions for new units were closed (this includes petitions where no election was ever held), with 56.37% closed with a certification order, creating 482 total new units. This was a -56.65% decrease from the previous year in successful union certifications. Overall this represents approximately 45,433 workers, which is a -19.24% decrease from the previous year The median bargaining unit has 20 workers.
