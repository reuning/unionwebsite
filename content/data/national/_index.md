##  United States

Excluding public employees and multi-union elections, in the last 12 months there have been 2,073 union elections filed in the United States and 1,607 union elections held. In 1,055 of those elections a new unit was certified. There are currently 568 open representation cases and 334 of are still waiting to vote.
